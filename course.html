Our Courses

Course We Offer

BBA

The BBA program aims to develop candidates that possess administrative skills, analytical skills, problem-solving tools, and the professionalism required to run an organization effectively and efficiently in the corporate world. The program is designed to give a broad knowledge in the functional areas of the company and their interconnections. The program exposes students to a variety of “core subjects” and allows students to specialize in a specific area of management.The degree also develops the student’s practical managerial skills, communication skills, and business decision-making capability. The program incorporates academic and practical experience, in the form of case projects, presentations, internships, industrial visits, and interaction with experts from the industry. This program develops executives and managers who not only carry out and implement policies and plans made by top-level management but also be involved in decision making. The program encourages students to become entrepreneurs and job creators.

BBIS

Information and its management are at the heart of every business today. The Bachelor of Business Information System (BBIS) is a program whereby information management and business disciplines are integrated and with the development in information systems and technologies, the students will be positioned for new career opportunities.The objective of the BBIS program is to prepare students to become competent, skilled, confident, and socially responsive management professionals with sound knowledge, abilities, and skills in the information system. The program helps students learn how to use and manage information technologies to revitalize business processes, conduct electronic commerce, improve business decision making, and gain a competitive advantage in today’s global market as well-bred independent managers of people and resources.

BHM

BHM is an internationally recognized four-year degree program affiliated to Kathmandu University. The program prepares students to take middle managerial and entrepreneurial positions in hotels, restaurants, travel and trekking agencies, amusement parks, destination management and marketing companies, event management firms, clubs, etc. in Nepal and abroad.The program emphasizes developing both technical and soft skills necessary to succeed in the hospitality and tourism industry in the 21st century. It provides students with an opportunity for learning, gaining exposure, and networking in the industry through its Internship program (1-year internship in the BHM program). It offers a wide range of concentration and elective courses to match students’ individual interests with the demand of the employment market.
